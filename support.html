Retro Matrix Phone — Support

How to Use

  • Open the slider: Tap the trigger button (top-left of slider) or swipe down on the slider cover
  • Close the slider: Tap the grip area at the bottom of the phone
  • Navigate menus: Use the scroll wheel (center button) to scroll up/down, press to select
  • Soft keys: The two buttons below the screen — left and right actions shown on screen
  • Type text: Default mode is Abc (multi-tap). Press a key repeatedly to cycle letters. Press # to switch modes (Abc → 123 → T9)
  • Delete items: In messages, notes, or call log — press Options then Delete
  • Easter eggs: Dial secret codes on the home screen keypad!

Frequently Asked Questions

The app is stuck / not responding

Go to Settings → Reset phone. This performs a factory reset and reboots the phone.

How do I type letters?

The default mode is Abc (multi-tap). Press a number key repeatedly to cycle through its letters. For example, press 2 once for 'a', twice for 'b', three times for 'c'. Wait 1 second for the letter to confirm, then press the next key.

How do I switch to T9 predictive text?

Press the # key to cycle between input modes: Abc (multi-tap) → 123 (numbers) → T9 (predictive).
